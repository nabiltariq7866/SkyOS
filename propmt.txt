# AI-Powered Air Rights, Drone Corridor & Municipal Airspace Intelligence Platform

 🎯 Demo Objective

Demonstrate a next-generation operating platform that helps SkyTrade move beyond an air-rights marketplace into a full low-altitude airspace intelligence, governance, pricing, and transaction infrastructure layer.

The demo should show how SkyTrade can:

· Turn air rights, drone corridors, property ownership, municipal rules, Remote ID/radar signals, and marketplace activity into one intelligent operating system
· Help municipalities govern, approve, price, monitor, and monetize low-altitude airspace safely
· Help property owners understand the value of their airspace and earn from approved usage
· Help drone operators discover compliant routes, price airspace access, request permissions, and reduce operational risk
· Improve liquidity and price discovery across air-rights parcels using AI-driven valuation models
· Add stronger compliance, auditability, route-risk scoring, smart-contract settlement, and municipal reporting
· Create a more technical, defensible infrastructure layer than a basic marketplace or static map
